Chicken Fried Rice

Description

This chicken friend rice recipe is very easy and tasty! it's basically cooked chicken, rice, soy sauce, and shredded egg stir-fried together. For best results, use leftover cooked rice.

Chicken Fried Rice Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cups cooked white rice, cold
  • 1 cup cooked, chopped chicken
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients

Step 2

Beat egg and water together in a small bowl. Melt butter in a large skillet or wok over medium-low heat add egg and cook without stirring, 1 to 2 minutes. Remove egg from skillet and cut into shreds; set aside.

Step 3

Heat oil in the same skillet. Add onion; cook and stir until soft, about 3 minutes. Stir in rice, chicken, soy sauce, and pepper. Cook and stir for about 5 minutes; stir in shredded egg. Serve hot.
