Best Steak Marinade in Existence

Description

Why Marinade a steak?

Marinating seasons the meat, while tenderizing it to enhance texture as well as flavour. The Marinating process also promotes browning.

We're not exaggerating with the title. This truly is the best steak marinade recipe in existence.

Steak Marinade Ingredients

  • Oil: This top-rated steak marinade recipe starts with olive oil, which keeps the meat nice and moist.
  • Sauces: You'll need Worchestershire sauce and soy sauce. Hot sauce is optional, but it's a great addition for people who appreciate a little heat.
  • Lemon juice: The acidity from the lemon juice cuts through the other flavours, adding welcome brightness. The acid also helps tenderize the meat even further.
  • Spices and seasonings: This flavourful marinade is seasoned with dried basil, garlic powder, dried parsley flakes, white pepper, and minced garlic.

How Long to Marinate

  • Thinner cuts, such as skirt or flanks steaks, should be marinated for 2-4 hours.
  • Tougher cuts, such as top sirloin or sirloin tip, will benefit from marinating overnight (about 8 hours).

How to Use Steak Marinate

Pour the marinade into a resealable plastic bag. Add steak, coat with the marinade, squeeze out excess air, and seal the bag. Marinate in the fridge for up to 8 hours. Remove steak from the marinate and shake of excess. Discard the remaining marinade. Cook or grill steaks to desired doneness.

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 cup olive oil
  2. 1/3 cup soy sauce
  3. 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
  4. 1/4 cup Worchestershire sauce
  5. 3 tablespoons dried basil
  6. 1 1/2 tablespoons garlic powder
  7. 1 1/2tablespoons dried parsley flakes
  8. 1 teaspoon dried minced garlic (Optional)
  9. 1/4 teaspoon hot pepper sauce (Optional)

Directions

Step 1

Place olive oil, soy sauce, lemon juice, Worchestershire sauce, basil, garlic powder, parsley, white pepper, minced garlic, and hot pepper sauce in a blender; blend on high speed until thoroughly combined, about 30 seconds.
